# FAQ

## Do you work with international clients?
Yes, 100%. I work remotely with clients worldwide. All communication is async-friendly via email, Slack, or WhatsApp.

## What is your typical response time?
Within 24 hours on weekdays. I typically reply same-day during business hours (10 AM – 6 PM IST).

## Do you do hourly or fixed-price projects?
Both. For well-defined projects, fixed-price is preferred. For ongoing work or consulting, hourly rates apply.

## Can you work with an existing codebase?
Yes. I regularly join projects mid-way, do code reviews, add features, or fix bugs in existing systems.

## Do you provide source code?
Always. The full source code is delivered on project completion via a private GitHub repository.

## What if I need changes after delivery?
2 weeks of bug fixes are included for free. Feature additions after delivery are billed separately.

## Do you sign NDAs?
Yes, happy to sign NDAs before sharing project details.

## Can you handle the full project alone or do I need a team?
For most projects under $10,000, I handle everything solo. For larger projects, I have trusted collaborators (designers, additional developers) I can bring in.

## What technologies do you NOT work with?
I focus on JavaScript/TypeScript ecosystem. I don't typically take native mobile (iOS/Android), PHP, or .NET projects.
