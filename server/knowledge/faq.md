# Frequently Asked Questions

## Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Skyline Web Co works with businesses, startups, and entrepreneurs worldwide. We operate remotely and communicate through email, Slack, Google Meet, WhatsApp, and other collaboration tools.

## What is your typical response time?

We typically respond to inquiries on the same business day. For active projects, communication is maintained regularly to ensure smooth progress and quick issue resolution.

## Do you offer fixed-price or hourly projects?

We offer both.

* Fixed-price projects for clearly defined scopes
* Hourly or monthly retainers for ongoing development, maintenance, and support

We'll recommend the most suitable model based on your project's requirements.

## Can you work with an existing codebase?

Absolutely. We can join ongoing projects, review existing codebases, fix bugs, implement new features, improve performance, and help modernize legacy applications.

## Do you provide the source code?

Yes. Clients receive full ownership of the source code and project assets upon completion unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## What happens after project delivery?

We provide post-launch support to ensure everything runs smoothly. Ongoing maintenance, updates, and feature development can also be arranged through a support plan.

## Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. We're happy to sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) before discussing sensitive business information or project requirements.

## What types of projects do you work on?

We specialize in:

* Business websites
* Startup MVPs
* SaaS applications
* Custom web applications
* Internal business tools
* E-commerce platforms
* API development and integrations

## What technologies do you work with?

Our primary stack includes:

* React.js
* Next.js
* Node.js
* Express.js
* MongoDB
* PostgreSQL
* Python
* FastAPI
* Flask
* Django

We select technologies based on project requirements rather than forcing a one-size-fits-all approach.

## How long does a project usually take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity.

* Landing pages: 1-2 weeks
* Business websites: 2-4 weeks
* Startup MVPs: 4-8 weeks
* Custom web applications: Timeline determined after project discovery

A detailed timeline is provided before development begins.

## How do we get started?

Simply contact us with your project requirements. We'll discuss your goals, recommend the best solution, provide a proposal, and outline the next steps for development.
